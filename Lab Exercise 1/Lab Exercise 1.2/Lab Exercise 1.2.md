This circuit uses an Arduino Uno to control multiple LEDs connected on a breadboard. Each LED is connected to a digital output pin through a resistor, which limits current and protects the LEDs. The Arduino can turn the LEDs on and off in different patterns, making the circuit useful for learning basic electronics and Arduino programming such as blinking or running light effects.
